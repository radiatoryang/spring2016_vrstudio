VR Studio, Spring 2016

Virtual reality projects and research at NYU Game Center.

About this course

This course is a critical exploration of “virtual reality” (VR) as a passing fad, dystopian nightmare, and new mode of technological consciousness. How do we reconcile the VR industry's promise of “presence” with existing discourse about immersion and realism in games? Classroom lectures and lab time will focus on prototyping experiences for VR, and critiquing VR as a media culture.
